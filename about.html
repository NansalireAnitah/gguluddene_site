About Us

Guluddene Traditional Healers and Herbalists Association

The Guluddene Traditional Healers and Herbalists Association is a premier non-governmental organization (NGO) in Uganda dedicated to uniting, mobilizing, and regulating traditional healers and herbalists. Fully registered with the Registrar of Companies under registration number 80020001377660, we are a legally recognized entity with members who have been thoroughly scrutinized by the National Executive Committee.

Led by President Mr. Jemba Godfrey Lumiramayengo, we draw leadership from the grassroots level—Parish, Sub-county, District, to the National level—ensuring a strong, community-rooted organization. Our mission is to preserve, protect, and promote Uganda’s herbal medicine and cultural heritage.


Our Aims

  • To unify and empower traditional healers and herbalists in Uganda to practice with professionalism and credibility.
  • To promote the integration of traditional medicine into Uganda’s national healthcare system.
  • To safeguard the cultural heritage and sacred sites associated with traditional healing practices.

Our Objectives


  • Regulation and Certification: Establish and enforce standards for traditional healers and herbalists through certification programs.
  • Training and Education: Provide workshops, training sessions, and resources to enhance the skills and knowledge of practitioners.
  • Advocacy and Policy Influence: Lobby for government recognition and support, including land allocation for medicinal plant conservation and inclusion in Uganda’s Vision 2040.
  • Environmental Conservation: Protect sacred sites and promote sustainable harvesting and cultivation of medicinal plants in response to climate change challenges.

About Us

We are committed to preserving traditional healing practices and Uganda’s rich cultural heritage through training, advocacy, and community engagement.
