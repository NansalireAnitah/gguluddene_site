Our Vision

A respected, united community of traditional healers and herbalists contributing to Uganda’s health and cultural heritage, recognized nationally and globally for excellence and authenticity.

What We Do

Certification Programs

Certify and regulate practitioners to maintain high standards and eliminate fraudulent practices.

Training Initiatives

Workshops and capacity-building programs to enhance expertise in traditional healing.

Advocacy Efforts

Collaborate with government and international organizations for traditional medicine support.

Conservation Projects

Protect sacred sites and promote the sustainable use of medicinal plants.

Public Outreach

Raise awareness about traditional healing, combat HIV/AIDS, and address social injustices.

Leadership

Under the dynamic leadership of President Mr. Jemba Godfrey Lumiramayengo, Chairperson of the Re-Union of Traditional Healers and Herbalists Association, we are guided by unity, professionalism, and cultural pride. His grassroots-to-national approach ensures our initiatives resonate with communities across Uganda.
